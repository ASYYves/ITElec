Tagaytay City, Cavite
September 30, 2025 | SVD Farm
A quick getaway from the bustling urban city.

Tagaytay is known to be a quick and easy spot to visit during the weekends and holidays. The of approximately 32km is just the right amount of throttle therapy and relaxation to relieve you from school or corporate stress. Various tourist spots from Palace in the sky, SVD Farm, Picnic Grove, Mahogany Market, and etc always welcome both locals and tourists in their care. It's up to you to choose which spot will fill your adventurous needs.
Maragondon, Cavite
October 10, 2025 | Kaybiang Tunnel
The Riders Destination of the South.

Kaybiang tunnel is the so called Riders Destination, as the road to get there is a beginner friendly road for sprouting riders. Cavite often have easy going roads, and a lot of straightaways that makes the ride smooth and peaceful. The only winding road that you'll experience is at the end, before reaching Kaybiang tunnel. Make sure to gas up before making your way up, as there are no gas station available further up the road. will ensure that you'll have a safe and hassle free ride. With that in mind, the road up the mountain greets you with cold breeze, greenish ambient scent that revitalizes you making a "nature trip" experience. You'll get a chance to encounter native wildlife prior to arriving at the tunnel, which symbolizes the co-existence of nature and infrastructure.

BATANGAS

Laurel, Batangas
September 30, 2025 | Simbahang Bato
A scenic ride to a sacred destination.

Sharing the same route to Tagaytay, we'll head down to the winding road of Sampaloc Road. The scenic drive offers a unique refreshing experience as the cool breeze and green floods your five senses as you cruise down the road. A couple of spots offers very photogenic scenes that can prove to be Instagram worthy even for your pickiest followers. But going down further, we exit to Talisay, and head our way to Laurel, Batangas. Even though the cool breeze no longer greets us, what took its place is the majestic view of Taal Lake, and Taal Volcano that makes the trip worth it. Aside from the quiet and peaceful atmosphere, Simbahang Bato gives off a serene feeling that contributes to sacred value as a place of worship.

Lobo, Batangas
November 15, 2025 | Malagundi Point
A hidden gem at the farthest point of the South

This trip is not for the light hearted, as this ride not only challenges the rider's ability, but also the rider's vehicle. Always ensure to pack properly and make sure that the motorcycle is in good condition before setting out. The road to Malagundi Point offers various types of roads and road hazards that keeps the driver on edge. However, you'll get the chance to visit various tourist destination, before reaching Malagundi Point. The final stretch of the route is a lengthy offroad segment that challenges both the rider and the vehicles capabilities. At the end of it all, it rewards you with the sound of the crashing waves, and humid breeze of the beach. A true hidden gem away far away from the urban city and the farthest point of the south.

RIZAL

Baras, Rizal
November 7, 2025 | MARILAQUE Highway
From the busy streets of the metro to the windy mountain road.

The road to Marilaque stretches from Marikina, Rizal, Laguna and Quezon. Hence, the name Marilaque. You'll go through the busy streets of Muntinlupa to the gruelling traffic of Taguig. But that's the beauty of this trip is that after a hellish drive, it rewards you at the end with winding roads with beautiful trees and picture perfect spots that pleases the eyes. Though Marilaque holds the status of one of the accident prone spots in the Philippines, it is imperative that when traveling there always practice defensive driving.
